Pili Pinas

We are a community of nation builders. The Titibayan Program is a community of Filipinos dedicated to love for country, art and music.

Ang Titibayan Program ay pa-uso ng grupong BandITO – yes capital ITO for IT Outsourcing. Ang BandITO ay binubuo ng mga musiko na kasalukuyang empleyado at stressed sa trabaho, pero hindi naman galit. Good vibes lang!

Kung interesado kayo makisama at makigulo, e-mail lang titibayan2020@gmail.com
